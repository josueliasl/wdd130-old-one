The Holy Grail

    
    BYU-Idaho The Church

Article 1

Various traditions describe the Holy Grail as a cup, dish, or stone with miraculous healing powers, sometimes providing eternal youth or sustenance in infinite abundance, often guarded in the custody of the Fisher King and located in the hidden Grail castle.

Article 2

According to the teachings of Christianity, the Holy Grail was the cup that Jesus Christ drank from during the Last Supper The search for the Holy Grail became the principal quest of the knights of King Arthur.
