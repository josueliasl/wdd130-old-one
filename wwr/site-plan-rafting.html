Overview

Purpose

Learn to maneuver and navigate the raft through rapids or turbulent waters, and enjoy this unique time with those you love. Most parts of rivers, usually the most beautiful parts, are hard to reach on land. Rafting allows you to enter these special places. Imagine dense forests, nature, great views, and much more. Even though we live in houses, flats, and big cities we truly belong in nature.

Audience

Who are the target customers? This website is intended to help college students who enjoy traveling with their friends or who are already young matrimonies. They are usually between 18 to 30 years old, usually, they are tired and sick of the classroom and office. They want to enjoy a good weekend seeing the wonders of nature. Their financial situation is middle-class while they work in university. What do they want? They desire to relax from the stress and pressure of the city, teachers, or office. They want to accumulate experiences and live adventures. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? They will usually do it from a Laptop or mobile device.
